About Institute

MD Ahsan Habib Principal

Leading with vision and dedication since 2010. Under his guidance, TBMI has become a centre of excellence in technical education.

Technical and Business Management Institute was established in 2004 with the aim of developing skilled and knowledgeable professionals in the field of technology and business education. The institute provides quality technical education and focuses on practical learning to prepare students for modern careers.

Mission: To provide high quality technical and business education that prepares students with practical skills, innovation, and professionalism.
Vision: To become a leading technical institute in Bangladesh by producing skilled graduates ready for the modern workforce.
